6th Convocation PUGC

  • Date: December 24, 2022
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Location: University of the Punjab, Gujranwala Campus

Description

The 6th Convocation was held at the Punjab University Gujranwala Campus on Saturday, December 24, 2022. Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Asghar Zaidi Sahib, the ceremony's chief guest, and Prof. Dr. Naveed Iqbal Chaudhary, the director general, expressed their gratitude to the participants and their families. The VC emphasised the importance of learning about and adhering to Allama Iqbal's life and his Shaheen's philosophy. He urged the young graduates to assume leadership roles. The students received congratulations and best wishes from Dr. Naveed Iqbal Chaudhry. He urged the young people to work hard to better Pakistan's future. Over 500 degrees were awarded to students, and those who placed first each received a medal. The Director General gave the campus development report and expressed his profound gratitude to the faculty and staff at the Gujranwala Campus, particularly Professor Aamir Sohail, who is in charge of the Examinations Department, and Madam Adila Hussain, stage secretary. At the ceremony's completion, guests and officials received honorary shields. Among the distinguished visitors from Lahore were Professor Dr. Zulfikar Bora, Professor Dr. Sajid Rashid Gujar, Professor Dr. Azhar Naeem, Professor Dr. Shehzad Sarwar, Professor Dr. Ehsan Malik, Professor Dr. Mudassar Ghafoor, Mr. Raja Shahid javed, Professor Dr. Amjad Abbas Magsi and Mr Khalid Mehmood. Following the event, a delicious lunch was provided for all of the guests and students.
